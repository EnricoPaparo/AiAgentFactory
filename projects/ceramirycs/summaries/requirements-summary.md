# Requirements Summary: ceramirycs

## Source

- `blueprints/requirements-blueprint.md`
- Approved by `human-gates/approve-requirements.md`

## Runtime Summary

CeraMirycs needs a minimal two-page website for Miriam's cozy ceramic productions. The site must feel modern, warm, artisanal and clean. It needs a landing page, a contact page, navigation between them and Italian copy.

## Hard Constraints

- Do not invent realistic contact data.
- Contact fields must include email, telefono, indirizzo and Instagram.
- Every contact value must be empty or `da definire`.
- No ecommerce, backend, CMS, newsletter, analytics, booking, deployment or product catalog.

## Acceptance Focus

- Landing page clearly names CeraMirycs.
- Contact page lists the required fields.
- Local inspection is possible.
- Final review confirms the four Human Gates and pipeline steps were respected.
